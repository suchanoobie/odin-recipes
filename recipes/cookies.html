Description

This is another one of my favourites as they are so simple to make and you can easily change the recipe to swap the chocolate to raisins etc.

Ingredients

  • 120g Softend Butter
  • 75g Light Brown Sugar
  • 75g Golden Caster Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 180g Plain Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Bicarbonate Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 150g Dark Chocolate Cut into Chunks

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 180C and line 2 baking trays with baking parchment
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar until very light and fluffy, then beat in the egg and vanilla
  3. Then stir in the flour, bicarb and chocolate
  4. Scoop 10 large tbsps of the mixture onto the trays leaving enough space between them to allow for spreading
  5. Bake for 10-12 mins or until firm at the edges and soft in the middle
  6. Leave to cool, they will harden slightly as they cool down
