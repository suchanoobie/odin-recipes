Victoria Sponge

Description

This is a recipe to make a quick and simple Victoria Sponge cake.

Ingredients

  • 200g Caster Sugar
  • 200g Softened Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 200g Self Raising Flour

For The Filling

  • 100g Softened Butter
  • 140g Sifted Icing Sugar
  • Drop Of Vanilla Extract
  • 170g Strawberry Jam
  • Icing Sugar to Decorate

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 190C/fan 170C/gas 5. Butter two 20cm sandwich tins and line with non-stick baking paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl beat together the caster sugar, softened butter and eggs
  3. Fold in the flour
  4. Divide the mixture between the 2 tins and smooth with a spatula
  5. Bake for about 20 mins until golden and the cake springs back when pressed.
  6. Turn out onto a cooling rack and leave to cool
  7. To make the filling beat teh butter vuntil smooth and creamy, then gradually mix in the icing sugar and the drop of vanilla extract
  8. Spread the buttercream over the bottom of one of the sponges. Top it with 170g strawberry jam and sandwich the second sponge on top.
  9. Dust the top woth a little icing sugar before serving. Keep in an airtight container and eat within 2 days
